Kobe Bryant was an American Basketball Player.
He is heralded as one of the best players of all time, had one of the greatest work ethics and was committed to achieving greatness.

    Achievements

  • 2x Finals MVP
  • 5x NBA Champion
  • 2008 MVP
  • 2x Olympic gold medalist
  • 18x All Star
  • 2x Scoring Champion
  • 12x All Defence
  • 33,643 career points

1996 Kobe is drafted with the 13th pick at age 17

1997 Kobe makes his debut in the summer league

1997 Kobe wins the dunk contest

1999-2002 kobe wins three championships back to back

2006 Bryant scores a career high of 81 points

2008 Kobe wins his fourth championship and wins olympic gold

2010 Kobe wins his fifth championship

2012 Kobe wins his second olympic gold medal

At 9:45am on the 26th of January 2020 Kobe, his daughter Gianna and 7 others tragically lost their lives in a helicopter crash.
